Check your data

The data we have collected will appear on a national map of brownfield land.

Thoroughly check the data in your most recent register. By clicking on the version above, you can view a breakdown and visualisation of the data. In particular, you should check the locations of the sites on the map, and the number of dwellings and size of the sites.

What to do if any data is wrong

On the view your data page, download the processed .csv file and correct the data.

Once you have fixed any errors you need to upload the .csv file to your website and let us know the URL.

We are working on a tool that will help you check your changes before you upload the file to your website.
